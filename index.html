    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: May 21, 2026

    1. Data Collection & Storage

    PulseTime Pro is a "privacy-by-design" application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data—including timers, alarms, and theme settings—is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage.

    2. No Tracking

    We do not use tracking pixels, invasive analytics, or third-party cookies to monitor your behavior. Your productivity is your business.

    3. Cookies

    PulseTime Pro does not use traditional cookies. We use LocalStorage to remember your preferences between sessions.

    4. Contact

    Since we do not collect any data, we have no way to contact you. If you have questions, please refer to our GitHub repository for support.
